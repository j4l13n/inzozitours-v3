11 days full of Congo Nile Trail

Overview

Embark on an 11-day journey along Rwanda's Congo Nile Trail, exploring 10 sections spanning 227 kilometers. This trail offers diverse landscapes, cultural encounters, and challenging terrain.

  • Language: English, French
  • Cost: 1500 USD per person (includes accommodation, meals, guiding)
  • Capacity: Any number of people
  • Duration: 7 hours per day on average
Accommodation and Meals

Accommodations along the trail range from budget guesthouses to upscale lodgings, providing options to suit all preferences. Each day includes breakfast, lunch, and dinner, offering a taste of local cuisine.

  • Accommodation options: Cyimbiri Guest House, Kinunu Guest House, Bumba Base Camp, Dian Fossey Hotel, Karongi hotels, Karengera High School, Kumbya Retreat Centre, Ishara Beach Hotel, St. Martin Parish
  • Meals: Breakfast, lunch, and dinner provided daily
