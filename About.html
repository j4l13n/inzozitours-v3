Vision

The vision of Inzozi Tours is to create awareness of the stunning wildlife that Rwanda has to offer, whilst creating a professional atmosphere to give our clients the best experience they have ever had. Inzozi Tours also seeks to promote tourism activities by involving the local communities.

Mission

Our vision at Inzozi Tours is to showcase Rwanda breathtaking wildlife while ensuring our clients receive unparalleled experiences in a professional environment. We also aim to engage local communities in promoting tourism activities, fostering sustainable growth and mutual benefit.

About

INZOZI, meaning 'dream' in our language, strives to turn your dreams into reality.
Embark on adventures of a lifetime with INZOZI Tours, exploring Rwanda diverse landscapes, from the lush forests of Volcanoes Mountains to the vast plains of Akagera National Park.

We pride ourselves on delivering unique and personalized experiences, allowing you to discover the beauty of Rwanda and its abundant wildlife.
